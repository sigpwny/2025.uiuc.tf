Mission Objective

You and your space crew will traverse star system UIUCTF 2025 to explore its various celestial bodies. Missions of types PWN, REV, CRYPTO, OSINT, WEB, and MISC will be assigned to your crew upon arrival.

Mission Parameters

STA 2025-07-26 00:00 UTC DUR 48H FMT Jeopardy CRW Unlimited Team Size

Directives

  1. Don't cheat.
    Each person can only compete on one team. Sharing flags or solves between teams will lead to immediate disqualification.
  2. Don't attack the infrastructure.
    Unless otherwise specified, do not run brute-force or enumeration tools such as nmap or dirbuster.
  3. Don't flag hoard.
    Submit flags as you find them and don't submit all at once towards the end.

Prizes

  • 1st Place $2,048 USD
  • 2nd Place $1,024 USD
  • 3rd Place $512 USD
  • 4th to 5th Place (each) $256 USD
  • 6th to 10th Place (each) $128 USD
  • Writeup Prize Pool $1,500+ USD

Note: In order to be eligible for cash prizes totaling over $600, you must be a tax entity in the United States. If you are not, you can choose to split the prize and/or donate to charity.
